Our Services:

Olympic Lift Training

Olympic lifting is a discipline of
strength training with an emphasis
on the specific lifts performed at the
Olympic Games like the "snatch"
and "clean and jerk".

This training will be done with Coach Adam

Power Lifting

Power lifting is a form of competitive lifting
which consists of deadlifts, squats, and
bench presses.

This training will be done with Coach Donte

Sports Massage

Performance massages catered each specific
athlete to work in tandem with workout routines.

Administered by Coach Adam

Sports Training

Individual or team sports
training(lacrosse or softball). Individual
training allows for a more specified
training approach while
group training allows for teamwork practice.

Training done by Coach Jessica and Coach Anne
